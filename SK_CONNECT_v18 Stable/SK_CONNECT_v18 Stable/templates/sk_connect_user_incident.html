Youth Warning Wall

A reminder board for youth behavior — to guide, not shame. Warnings are posted so everyone can learn, reflect, and help each other do better in the community.

What is the Youth Warning Wall?

This wall shows recorded youth violations per barangay. It is not meant to embarrass anyone, but to track incidents, follow up, and remind everyone about proper behavior in the community.
